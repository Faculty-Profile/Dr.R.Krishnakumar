About Dr. R. Krishnakumar

Academic Biography

Dr. R. Krishnakumar is an accomplished Associate Professor in the PG & Research Department of Commerce, St. Joseph's College of Arts & Science (Autonomous), Cuddalore. With over 28 years of teaching experience, he has dedicated his academic career to teaching, research, student mentoring and institutional development. He earned his Ph.D. in Commerce from Bharathidasan University and has guided numerous doctoral and postgraduate scholars. His research focuses on Financial Management, Digital Finance, Financial Inclusion, Accounting, Consumer Behaviour, Marketing and Business Management.
