Academic Biography

Dr. R. Krishnakumar

Associate Professor of Commerce

Dr. R. Krishnakumar is an accomplished academician, researcher and mentor serving as Associate Professor in the PG & Research Department of Commerce at St. Joseph's College of Arts & Science (Autonomous), Cuddalore. With more than twenty-eight years of teaching experience, he has established himself as a distinguished educator through excellence in teaching, research, academic leadership and student mentorship. His academic journey includes a Ph.D. in Commerce, M.Phil., MBA, M.Com., PGDFM and B.Com., reflecting his commitment to continuous learning and professional development.

Professional Overview

Throughout his academic career, Dr. R. Krishnakumar has actively contributed to higher education through classroom teaching, research supervision, curriculum development, editorial responsibilities, conference participation and university academic services. His research interests include Financial Management, Digital Finance, Financial Inclusion, Consumer Behaviour, Accounting and Business Management. He has published extensively in reputed national and international journals, including Scopus-indexed publications, and continues to guide scholars pursuing advanced research in Commerce.
